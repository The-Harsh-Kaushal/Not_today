About


Welcome to Not Today , the ultimate web-based game designed to train people of all ages in the art of surviving disasters! Whether you're facing a natural calamity, a man-made crisis, or simply want to be prepared for the unexpected, this game is your virtual survival guide.

Description


Not Today is an immersive, educational, and action-packed web-based game that combines thrilling gameplay with valuable disaster preparedness training. In this game, players will embark on an epic journey to overcome various disaster scenarios, from earthquakes and hurricanes to wildfires and pandemics. Your mission is to emerge not just as a survivor but as a true master of disaster preparedness.

Why Not Today

  • Prepare for real-life emergencies while having fun.
  • Learn valuable life skills in a safe and engaging environment.
  • Challenge yourself and compete with friends to become a disaster survival expert.
  • Contribute to a safer and more resilient community by spreading knowledge about emergency preparedness.
